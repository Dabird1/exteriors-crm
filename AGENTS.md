\# AGENTS.md



\## Project Overview

This repository is for building a modern CRM for an exteriors home services company (roofing, siding, windows, gutters, solar).



The goal is to create a practical, usable MVP that supports sales reps, operations, and management.



\---



\## Core Modules (MVP Scope)

\- Leads

\- Contacts / Customers

\- Inspections / Appointments

\- Estimates / Opportunities

\- Jobs / Production

\- Pipeline / Status tracking

\- Activities / Notes

\- Basic dashboard



\---



\## Business Context

This CRM is designed for real-world field usage:

\- Sales reps working in homes and neighborhoods

\- Mobile-first usage during inspections

\- Fast data entry and simple workflows

\- Clear pipeline visibility from lead → job completion



\---



\## Product Priorities (in order)

1\. Lead capture and management

2\. Inspection scheduling

3\. Sales pipeline tracking

4\. Estimate creation and tracking

5\. Job conversion and production handoff

6\. Basic reporting/dashboard



\---



\## Working Principles

\- Keep the architecture simple and maintainable

\- Avoid overengineering or enterprise complexity

\- Prefer speed to usable MVP over perfection

\- Build in small, incremental steps

\- Do not modify unrelated files

\- Explain major changes before making them

\- Keep UI clean and mobile-friendly

\- Use clear, business-friendly naming



\---



\## Technical Preferences

\- Use TypeScript where practical

\- Prefer widely adopted, stable frameworks

\- Keep local development simple

\- Minimize unnecessary dependencies

\- Structure code for readability and future scaling



\---



\## Data Model Guidance (High-Level)

Typical entities should include:

\- Lead

\- Contact / Customer

\- Property / Address

\- Inspection

\- Estimate / Opportunity

\- Job

\- User (sales rep, admin)

\- Activity / Notes



Each entity should support timestamps, status, and ownership.



\---



\## UX Expectations

\- Fast and simple forms

\- Minimal clicks for common actions

\- Mobile-friendly layouts

\- Clear status indicators (pipeline stages)

\- Easy navigation between related records



\---



\## What NOT to Do

\- Do not build unnecessary features outside MVP scope

\- Do not introduce complex microservices architecture

\- Do not refactor large parts of the codebase without explanation

\- Do not add heavy dependencies without justification



\---



\## How to Work on This Repo

When given a task:

1\. Understand the goal and relevant files

2\. Propose a plan if the change is non-trivial

3\. Make small, focused changes

4\. Explain what was done and why

5\. Suggest next steps if helpful



\---



\## Output Expectations

\- Keep responses clear and structured

\- Show file changes when relevant

\- Prefer incremental improvements over large rewrites
